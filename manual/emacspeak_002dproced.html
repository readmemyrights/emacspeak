12.148 emacspeak-proced ¶

PROCED == Process Editor A new Task Manager for Emacs. Proced is part of emacs 23.

Required modules

  • Emacspeak-Proced Commands

12.148.1 Emacspeak-Proced Commands ¶

  • emacspeak-proced-jump-to-process
  • emacspeak-proced-next-field
  • emacspeak-proced-next-line
  • emacspeak-proced-previous-field
  • emacspeak-proced-previous-line
  • emacspeak-proced-speak-args
  • emacspeak-proced-speak-field
  • emacspeak-proced-speak-that-field
  • emacspeak-proced-speak-this-field

12.148.1.1 emacspeak-proced-jump-to-process ¶

Command: emacspeak-proced-jump-to-process (name) ¶
Jump to process by name.

(fn NAME)

12.148.1.2 emacspeak-proced-next-field ¶

Command: emacspeak-proced-next-field ¶
Navigate to next field.

12.148.1.3 emacspeak-proced-next-line ¶

Command: emacspeak-proced-next-line ¶
Move to next line and speak a summary.

12.148.1.4 emacspeak-proced-previous-field ¶

Command: emacspeak-proced-previous-field ¶
Navigate to previous field.

12.148.1.5 emacspeak-proced-previous-line ¶

Command: emacspeak-proced-previous-line ¶
Move to next line and speak a summary.

12.148.1.6 emacspeak-proced-speak-args ¶

Command: emacspeak-proced-speak-args ¶
Speak command  invocation  for this process.

12.148.1.7 emacspeak-proced-speak-field ¶

Command: emacspeak-proced-speak-field (field-name) ¶
Speak value of specified field in current line.

(fn FIELD-NAME)

12.148.1.8 emacspeak-proced-speak-that-field ¶

Command: emacspeak-proced-speak-that-field ¶
Speak desired field via single keystroke.

12.148.1.9 emacspeak-proced-speak-this-field ¶

Command: emacspeak-proced-speak-this-field (&optional position) ¶
Speak field at specified column — defaults to current column.

(fn &optional POSITION)
